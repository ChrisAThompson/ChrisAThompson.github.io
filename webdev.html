In my Web Desgin course, we had to choose a topic and make a website that was related to our topic. As you can see my topic was about the importance and risks of mobile security. For this assignment, the professor taught us how to create a parallax design for our website. I chose my own color scheme, also included visual graphs for statistical information, and a video about mobile security.
